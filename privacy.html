Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 10, 2026

Vice Lobby ("we," "our," or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect your information when you use the Vice Lobby mobile application.

Information We Collect

When you create an account or use Vice Lobby, we may collect:

  • Email address

  • Messages sent through the app

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Help you discover crews, friends, and matches
  • Provide messaging and social features
  • Maintain account security and prevent abuse

Data Storage

Your account information and content are securely stored using trusted third-party infrastructure, including Supabase, to provide authentication, database, and storage services.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share information with service providers when necessary to operate and secure Vice Lobby.

Third-Party Services

Vice Lobby may use third-party services that process data on our behalf, such as authentication, cloud storage, and infrastructure providers. These services have their own privacy policies.

Your Choices

You may update or delete your profile information within the app. If you wish to delete your account, you may do so through the app or contact us for assistance.

Children's Privacy

Vice Lobby is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
